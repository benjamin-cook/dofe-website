Duke of Edinburgh

The Duke of Edinburgh is an award scheme to get people across the UK to develop themselves and help their local communities. It has three categories; Volunteering, Skills and Physical, and a final expedition. You cannot have an assesor that is a direct family member. One section must be done for 6 months.

Volunteering

Volunteering is a way to get people to help their local communities in some way or another. You could do litter-picking, or working in a local charity shop, pretty much anything as long as you don't get paid for doing it.

Skills

Skills and Physical can sort of blend together on this one. It's a bit of a grey area but the gist is that as long as it develops your skills in something, then it counts as skills.It could be baking, cooking, or coding like me!
Physical

Physical section is literally anything sports-wise.

Expedition

2 days, 1 night spent out in the wild with a group of people who you probably know, doing cooking, navigation, and mostly, walking.
